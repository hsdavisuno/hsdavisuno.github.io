About Heather S. Davis




I am a full-time Sr. Learning Systems Analyst for Conagra Brands in Omaha (currently working from home) and a part-time MS MIS student with a concentration in data analytics. In my job, I manage the Workday Learning system for my employer. I have a Masters of Education in Educational Technology from Arizona State University and have worked in learning technologies for over 20 years.

With my second masters, I am looking to move into an even more technical role. This is my fourth semester at UNO. After this semester, I will be about half way through my degree.
